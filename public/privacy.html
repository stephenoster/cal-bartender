Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Collin Thomas ("the Service") is operated as a personal project. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our SMS-based cocktail recommendation service.

What we collect

  • Your phone number
  • The content of messages you send to the Service, including any photos of your bar or ingredients
  • Conversation history, which may be stored to personalize future sessions and remember your preferences

How we use it

Your phone number is used solely to send you replies. Your message content — including text and any photos you send — is used to generate personalized cocktail recommendations. Conversation history may be stored persistently to provide continuity across sessions, remember your preferences, and improve recommendations over time.

Sub-processors

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic — AI processing of your messages and photos to generate recommendations
  • Twilio — SMS delivery and phone number management

We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to any other third party. No mobile opt-in data or phone numbers will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Photos

If you send a photo of your bar or ingredients, it is processed by Anthropic's API for the sole purpose of generating cocktail recommendations. Photos are not stored permanently.

Opting out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages and delete your data from our system. Reply START to resume.

Contact

For questions about this policy, text the number directly.
